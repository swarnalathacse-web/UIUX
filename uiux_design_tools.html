UI/UX Design Tools

A quick-reference guide to the tools used in modern product design — from early sketches to handoff.

💡

How to read this guide

Tools are grouped by design phase. Each card shows the tool's main purpose, key features, and type. Most tools have a free student plan — start with Figma for prototyping and Miro for collaboration.

Phase 1 — Research & Planning
🗺️

Miro

miro.com

Infinite online whiteboard for brainstorming, user journey maps, and affinity diagrams. Great for team workshops.

Whiteboard Collaboration Free plan
📋

Notion

notion.so

All-in-one workspace for user research notes, personas, and design documentation. Good for team knowledge bases.

Documentation Research Free plan
📊

Google Forms

forms.google.com

Simple tool for creating user surveys and collecting quantitative data. Built-in analysis with Google Sheets.

Surveys Data Free
Phase 2 — Wireframing
✏️

Balsamiq

balsamiq.com

Intentionally rough, sketch-style wireframes that keep focus on layout and structure, not aesthetics.

Low-fidelity Wireframe Paid
🎨

Figma

figma.com

Industry-standard browser-based design tool. Supports wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, prototypes, and developer handoff — all in one place.

Wireframe Prototyping Handoff Free edu plan
📄

Whimsical

whimsical.com

Clean tool for flowcharts, mind maps, and quick wireframes. Very beginner-friendly with a minimal interface.

Flowcharts Mind maps Free plan
Phase 3 — Visual Design
🖌️

Figma

figma.com

Build pixel-perfect UI screens using components, design tokens, auto-layout, and shared styles. The go-to for high-fidelity mockups.

Components Auto-layout Free edu plan
🎭

Adobe XD

adobe.com/xd

Adobe's UI/UX tool for designing and prototyping. Integrates well with Photoshop and Illustrator for asset workflows.

Hi-fidelity Adobe Paid
🖼️

Canva

canva.com

Drag-and-drop visual design for banners, presentations, and social graphics. Not for app UI, but great for design assets and presentations.

Graphics Presentations Free plan
Phase 4 — Prototyping & Testing
▶️

Figma Prototype

figma.com

Create clickable, shareable prototypes directly from your Figma designs. Supports transitions, overlays, and interactive components.

Clickable Shareable Built into Figma
🧪

Maze

maze.co

Usability testing platform. Share your Figma prototypes with real users and get heatmaps, click data, and feedback automatically.

Usability testing Analytics Free plan
📱

InVision

invisionapp.com

Prototyping and design feedback tool. Upload screens, link them, and collect comments from stakeholders and users in one place.

Prototyping Feedback Freemium
Phase 5 — Developer Handoff
📐

Figma Dev Mode

figma.com

Inspect designs for exact spacing, font sizes, colors, and export assets. Generates code snippets for CSS, iOS, and Android automatically.

Inspect CSS export Built into Figma
🔗

Zeplin

zeplin.io

Dedicated handoff tool. Connects with Figma/Sketch and presents design specs, assets, and styleguides clearly to developers.

Specs Assets Free plan
